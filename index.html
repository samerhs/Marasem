Services

What We Do

Marasem
We are a high-quality epoxy coating supplier and installer dedicated to providing the best work on every project. We use A wide range of the latest and best technologies and materials. We then combine these technologies to produce reliable systems at affordable competitive cost.

Epoxy Flooring

Marasem can seamlessly work with your construction team to professionally install your epoxy flooring on time and on budget. Our expert technicians will shape any floor to your satisfaction.

Exterior & Interior Painting

Marasem is a trustworthy painting contractor based in Dubai, UAE. We are composed of experienced engineers and experienced workers.

Kerbstone & Wheel Stopper

Marasem is experienced in Kerbstones throughout Dubai and is known in the market to offer a quality products with expert workmanship. Our teams of highly trained and skilled.

Self-leveling concrete

Marasem is experienced in Self-leveling concrete which is polymer-modified cement that has high flow characteristics and, does not require the addition of excessive amounts of water for placement.

Gypsum False Ceilings

Marasem is not only a false ceiling contractor for businesses, but also creates modern, stylish gypsum board ceilings for the home.

Tyrolean

Marasem with its experienced workers can handle all types of Tyrolean works spraying it with a tyrolean gun, or in some cases, a top fed hopper gun powered by a compressor.
